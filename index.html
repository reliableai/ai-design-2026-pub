Designing Large Scale AI Systems

CS Master Course | Spring 2026 | 12 weeks

About This Course

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to designing, building, and evaluating large-scale AI systems. Students will gain hands-on experience building AI-powered applications and learn rigorous evaluation methodologies essential for deploying AI systems in production.

Basic software engineering experience is preferred — but not a must. The main prerequisite is the passion to build great systems.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will:

1. Build Effective, Reliable AI Systems at Scale

Solve complex problems by decomposing them in a principled way. Ship a reliable AI feature into an existing product.

2. Use AI to Build AI

Leverage AI for development, evaluation, and system management. Understand what this unlocks and where the limits are.

3. Iterate Towards Success

Master the science of monitoring, analysis, and estimation. Stand up an evaluation harness you can run in CI.

4. Identify and Master the Key Abstractions

Build observability, cost controls, and incident-ready guardrails. Take lessons from software engineering past, but do so critically.

5. Think in Systems

Understand which engineering principles carry forward and which new abstractions are essential.
